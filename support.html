Frequently Asked Questions

What is Recall Flow?

Recall Flow is an AI-powered flashcard app for iOS that uses smart spaced repetition to help you learn faster and remember longer. You can create your own cards, import Anki decks, or let AI generate flashcards from your notes and study materials.

How does the spaced repetition algorithm work?

Our algorithm is based on the proven Anki system. It tracks how well you know each card and schedules reviews at the optimal moment — right before you're about to forget. The AI also builds a personal memory model for each card, continuously adapting to your unique learning patterns.

Can I import my existing Anki decks?

Yes! Recall Flow supports importing Anki decks in one tap. Once imported, your cards are automatically enhanced with AI-powered scheduling and hints to supercharge your study sessions.

How does AI card generation work?

Simply paste your notes, a PDF, or a URL into Recall Flow. Our AI analyses the content, identifies key concepts, and instantly creates perfectly structured flashcards ready for review. You can always edit or customize the generated cards afterwards.

Is Recall Flow free to use?

Recall Flow is free to download with core features available at no cost. Some advanced features like unlimited AI card generation and premium analytics may require a subscription. No credit card is needed to get started.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your flashcard data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable cloud sync, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period, and you'll continue to have access until then.

Which platforms does Recall Flow support?

Recall Flow is currently available for iOS (iPhone and iPad). We're focused on delivering the best possible experience on Apple devices. Stay tuned for updates on future platform support.
